About Me

who am i

building things, solving problems

Hello! I am a recent graduate and budding developer based in Norwich and London.

During my time as an undergraduate studying Chemical Engineering at UCL, my analytical and technical abilities were happily preoccupied, but I found that my creative and logical faculties were going underutilised.

I soon discovered the world of software development and realised that this is what I'm meant to do, and I never looked back.

From there, I went on to pursue a Diploma in Full Stack Software Development, from which I acquired the very skills that I used to create the front and back end for this website, and more.

I have always enjoyed making things and I am excited to keep on learning and working on even bigger and better projects.


When not in front of my computer, you’ll find me in front of my piano or in front of my crafts box.

Coding, fitness, arts & crafts, instruments with strings… these are a few of my favourite things!
